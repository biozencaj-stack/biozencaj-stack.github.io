About the project

The Demo E-Commerce CMS is our internal platform — a store we built to show rather than tell: a storefront with catalogue and filters, customer accounts, a cart and NestPay payments, two languages and an admin panel where everything is edited without a developer.

The platform is the foundation of our custom shop projects: instead of starting from zero, clients get a proven base adapted to their range and processes — and here they can try it live.

Challenge

We needed a demo showing the full e-commerce spectrum — storefront with catalogue and checkout, customer accounts, and an admin CMS for products, promotions, banners and newsletters.

Solution

Built with Next.js 16 (App Router, React 19), PostgreSQL and Prisma ORM, NextAuth, next-intl (SR/EN), Tailwind CSS 4 and Radix UI. The admin covers catalogue, sales, CMS and role-based access.

Result

A fully working demo — storefront, accounts, admin CMS, card payments and localisation — ready to be tailored to clients.
